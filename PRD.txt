Project Overview

-The Print Management System or PMS helps the shop owner or manager of the printing shop record customer printing orders digitally 
instead of manual recording via notebook, etc.The system (backend API) accepts new orders, automatically calculates 
the total price, saves every order, and lets the staff view the orders later.

Problem Statement

- Orders are manually recorded, for example by hand in a notebook. Computation errors happen, probably due to 
manual calculation or the staff doing the math too quickly. Orders sometimes get lost when pages are torn or the notebook is misplaced.
Sometimes the same order is written twice by mistake, like when a customer comes back for a previous order or orders a new one.
It would be challenging to find the information fast.
This slows the shop, causes possible arguments with the customers, and possibly loses money from wrong pricing.

Objectives

- Calculate the total price correctly every time using the right price per page.
Save every order so nothing gets lost.
Let staff find any order quickly by its number or see recent orders.
Stop most calculation mistakes and duplicate entries.
Make taking and checking orders faster than writing in a notebook.

Users

- The main users are people that work at the counter. They handle orders, take orders from customers, tell the prices, and sometimes look for old orders.
- The shop owner or manager also uses the system. They check the orders at the end of the day or look for a specific order when needed.
- Customers do not use the system directly. Only the staff enters and views the orders.

Cost

-The system uses these fixed printing prices:
 - Black and white: ₱2 per page
 - Colored: ₱5 per page
 - Photo paper: ₱20 per page
